WHATEVER IT TAKES

Are you ready to do anything for your goal?
In an exciting third-person shooter storyline, you will find a world where solutions are not always simple.
Whatever It Takes is a story about sacrifice, about choice, about how far you are willing to go to achieve what you want.
Get ready for an emotional drama that will make you doubt your beliefs and question the value of everything.

- Mesmerizing atmosphere: Immerse yourself in a world
where reality mixes with nightmares and every step carries danger.
ARE YOU READY?
- Non-linear story: Your decisions influence the development of the plot
and determine the fate of the characters.
- Diverse locations: Travel through gloomy cities, mysterious laboratories
and lost hideouts.
